Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EventPulse

Does your platform help in event registration?
No, our platform does not provide full event registration. Instead, we offer RSVP management where attendees can confirm their attendance by providing basic details like name, email, and contact information. This streamlined approach focuses on gathering essential information without the complexity of full registration systems.
Does your platform help market our event?
No, our platform does not provide marketing services for events. Wevent is focused on event execution, attendee management, and analytics. We recommend using dedicated marketing platforms and social media channels to promote your events, while Wevent handles the operational and analytical aspects.
How is Wevent different from other event management platforms?
Wevent stands out by focusing on the complete event lifecycle - from execution to insights. Unlike traditional platforms that only handle registration or ticketing, we bridge the gap between event execution and performance analysis. Our real-time engagement tracking, comprehensive analytics comparing RSVP vs actual attendance, and seamless integration capabilities make data-driven decision making accessible to every event organizer. We prioritize actionable insights over complex features, helping you understand what truly matters: attendee engagement and event success.
How can attendees join an event?
Attendees can join events through a simple RSVP process. Once they receive an event invitation or link, they submit their basic details (name, email, phone) to confirm attendance. For in-person events, they'll receive a QR code for quick check-in at the venue. For virtual or hybrid events, they'll get access links and instructions to join via integrated platforms like Zoom. The entire process is designed to be quick and hassle-free, ensuring maximum participation.
